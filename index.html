Meet... Steve Lacy

Steve Lacy got his start as a guitarist in the R&B/Soul band 'The Internet'. Quickly he became noticed by the music world at large for his unconventional recording methods, later releasing an EP entirely recorded, produced, and mastered on an iPhone! Gradually he was able to make inroads into more main stream hip-hop/R&B circles through featuring on some songs by Tyler, the Creator and Syd (the lead singer of the Internet).
